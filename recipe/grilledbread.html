Grilled Sandwich

Shane Fisher

Servings:

2

5 likes
Like this recipe! Register to like recipes!

Description


I made it with my friends

Ingredients



6 bread Slice
1 bowl Boil potato and matar stuffing
As needed Butter
As needed tomato sauce

Steps



1. Take all bread slice remove the brown sides.
2. Take one slice apply butter and then potato stuffing cover with another bread.
3. Now keep it in electronic grilled maker apply butter both sides and grilled it.
4. Serve with sauce.
