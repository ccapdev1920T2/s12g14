Latte Coffee

Daisy Bright

Servings:

1

0 likes
Like this recipe! Register to like recipes!

Description

Ingredients

1 shot espresso
2 shots foamed milk (or more)
Optional: 1 shot of flavored simple syrup (preferably a popular latte flavor, or chocolate syrup or chocolate powder mixed into a paste with a little hot water)

Steps

1. Pull your espresso shot in your latte cup and add a shot of flavored syrup for a flavored latte, if using.
2. Using a spoon to retain the microbubbles on top of the steamed milk, pour the bottom 2/3 of the steamed milk from the steaming pitcher into the latte cup.
3. Top the latte with the remaining bubbles. (You can spoon them onto your drink if desired.)
4. If you'd like, feel free to add some latte art. Serve and enjoy!
