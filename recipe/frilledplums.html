Frilled Plums

Alisha Natasha

Servings:

2

0 likes
Like this recipe! Register to like recipes!

Description


This was taught to me by my mother. I hope you like it!

Ingredients



1 tablespoon canola oil
4 medium black plums, halved and pitted
2 large limes, zested {reserve juice for dressing}
1 head radicchio, sliced
1 head butter lettuce, sliced
8 oz. jicama, sliced thin
2 serrano chilies, sliced thin
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 tablespoons agave syrup
2 large limes, juiced
salt and pepper to taste

Steps



1. Oil the grates of your stove top or outdoor grill
2. preheat for 5 minutes on high
3. Sprinkle plums with a little salt and pepper and place cut side down on hot grill
4. leave in place for a minute or two until your have some nice grill marks and then set aside.
5. Toss the radicchio, butter lettuce, lime zest, chilies and jicama in a large bowl and set aside.
6. Meanwhile in a small bowl, whisk together all the lime vinaigrette ingredients.
7. Pour dressing over salad mixture and toss until coated, add grilled plums to the top and serve.
